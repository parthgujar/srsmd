Accommodation for up-to 55 people.
8 Rooms and 6 self-contained Kutirs.
Library rooms for study.
CD distribution room and Office.
Chitrashala depicting Param KrupaluDev’s JeevanCharitra.

Kartik Sud 5 : Gyan Panchmi. Worship and Pooja of Shastra.
Kartik Sud 15 : Birth Anniversary of Param KrupaluDev. Palkhi and Ulhas Bhakti.
Chaitra sud 13 : Birth Anniversary of Lord Mahavir.
Chaitra Vad 5 : Death Anniversary of Param KrupaluDev. Virah Bhakti.
Vaishakh Sud 8 : Death Anniversary of PrabhuShreeji (Shri Laghuraj Swami). Atmasiddhi Pooja and bhakti.
Ashadh Sud 14 : Bruhad Alochna.
Ashadh Sud 15 : Mandir Dwajha Aarohan and Atmasiddhi Pooja
Shravan Sud 13 to Bhadarva Sud 5 : 8 days programme of Paryushan Parva. Samvatsari - Atmasiddhi Pooja, Samvatsarik Pratikraman.
Aaso Vad 1 : Creation day of Atmasiddhi Shastra and birthday of Shri PrabhuShreeji (Shri Laghuraj Swami). Atmasiddhi Vandana.
Aaso Vad 13,14, Amas and Kartak Sud 1 : Samadhi Maran Mala.
Over and above, Atmasiddhi Shastra pooja on each Purnima and Jinendra Panch Kalyanak on each Ekam are held regularly at the mandir.

Maximum 8 days stay in the Ashram at a time.
It is imperivitve to attend daily bhakti and Swadhyay in Mandir, on time.
Follow bramhacharya.
Mobile use in temple premises is prohibited.
Adhere to the kitchen's schedule for Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner.
It's important to attire in decent clothing at all times in the Ashram.
